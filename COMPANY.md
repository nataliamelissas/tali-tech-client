# TaliTech Company Overview

## Mission & Values

TaliTech teaches K-12 students how to code through play, fun, and engaging project-based and game-based learning methods. We believe in giving kids choice and 21st century skills that will help them be the future leaders and creators of technology.

We prioritize the development of each child's potential, meeting them right where they're at, and focusing not just on their technical capabilities but also on their personal development as confident individuals. We strive to cultivate a strong community where every child feels included and has the tools to succeed.

### Core Principles
- Project-based and game-based learning
- Computational thinking and creativity development
- Technology literacy for all backgrounds
- Personalized learning paths
- Safe, inclusive environment

## Pricing & Policies

### Camp Pricing
- **Early Bird**: $249 per week (available until April 1, 2026)
- **Regular**: $279 per week (after April 1, 2026)
- **Supply Fee**: $35 per student
- **Aftercare**: $100 per week (4:00 PM - 6:00 PM, available for all weeks)

### Discounts
- **Multi-Week**: 10% off each student's 2nd (and subsequent) camp registration
- **Sibling**: Applied per student - each student gets 10% off their 2nd+ week independently
- **Discount Application**: Applied to the 2nd+ class per student (base price + supply fee)

#### Discount Examples
- **Single student, 2 weeks**: Week 1 ($284) + Week 2 ($255.60 after 10% off) = $539.60
- **2 siblings, 1 week each**: Sibling 1 ($284) + Sibling 2 ($284) = $568 (no discount - each only taking 1 week)
- **2 siblings, 2 weeks each**: 
  - Sibling 1: Week 1 ($284) + Week 2 ($255.60) = $539.60
  - Sibling 2: Week 1 ($284) + Week 2 ($255.60) = $539.60
  - Total: $1,079.20 (saved $56.80)

### Payment Terms
- **Full Payment**: Due before first day of camp
- **Installment Option**: Minimum $50 deposit to register, parent chooses split, remainder due by first day
- **Payment Methods**: 
  - **Online**: Square (credit/debit cards) - all transactions processed securely by Square
  - **In-Person**: Square or Venmo
- **Security**: Card information never stored by TaliTech - all processing through Square

### Cancellation & Aftercare
- **Aftercare**: Available 4:00-6:00 PM for all registered students, $100/week
- **Special Needs**: Additional aftercare needs determined case-by-case for additional fee
- **Curriculum**: Carefully tailored to developmental needs of each age group
